Our Story

From humble beginnings to serving the community

Bringing Quality Fast Food to Postmasburg

Founded by Cyrilene Assegaai, Eagle's Fast Food was born from a passion to bring high-quality fast food to our local community. What started as a small eatery has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our commitment to quality, flavor, and excellent service.

We combine international standards with local flavors to create unforgettable taste experiences. Every dish we serve is prepared with care, using fresh ingredients and recipes that have been perfected over time.

Our mission is simple: to serve delicious food that makes people happy. Whether it's our famous Big Boy Sandwiches or our catering platters, we put the same care and attention into every item we serve.

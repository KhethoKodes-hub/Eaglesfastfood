Our Story

Bringing international flavors to Postmasburg

Founded by Cyrilene Assegaai

Eagle's Fast Food was born from a passion to bring high-quality fast food to our local community. We combine international standards with local flavors to create unforgettable taste experiences.

From our famous Big Boy Sandwiches to our catering platters, every item is prepared with care, quality ingredients, and a commitment to excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your delivery options?

We offer delivery within a 5km radius of our Postmasburg location for orders over R150. For catering orders, we deliver throughout the Tsantsabane area with varying delivery fees based on distance.

How far in advance should I book catering?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for large events (50+ people) and at least 3 days in advance for smaller gatherings. However, we'll always try to accommodate last-minute requests when possible.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes! We have several vegetarian options on our regular menu and can create custom vegan menus for catering events. Just let us know about any dietary restrictions when you place your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments (SnapScan, Zapper). For catering orders, we require a 50% deposit to confirm your booking, with the balance due on delivery or pickup.

How can I apply for a franchise opportunity?

We'd love to discuss franchise opportunities with you! Please fill out our contact form selecting "Franchise Opportunity" as the service type, and our franchise development team will get back to you with more information.
